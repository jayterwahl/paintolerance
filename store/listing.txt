======================================

TITLE: Gom Yapper — Resilience Training for Your Timeline

SHORT DESCRIPTION (132 chars max):
Simulated hostile replies on your tweets to build immunity against internet scorn. No AI, no API keys, no data collection.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
The internet is full of bots and bad-faith reply guys trying to get a rise out of you. The Gom Yapper helps you build immunity.

This extension adds simulated hostile replies to your own tweets — mean, dismissive, off-topic, and completely fake. The replies come from generated personas and are produced entirely offline. No AI, no API keys, no data collection.

HOW IT WORKS
• Enter your Twitter/X handle in the extension popup
• Toggle the extension on
• Your tweets will appear to have attracted a wave of hostile, absurd, and completely fake replies
• Adjust intensity from Mild (3–5 replies) to Medium (6–10) to Unhinged (11–20)

8 REPLY ARCHETYPES
• Ratio Guy — one-word dismissals ("ratio", "L", "cope")
• Crypto Bro — derails everything into crypto promotion
• Pseudo-Intellectual — sneering condescension disguised as insight
• Unhinged Reply Guy — disproportionate emotional reactions
• The Bot — spam patterns and engagement farming
• Concern Troll — faux-sympathetic backhanded comments
• Off-Topic Ranter — completely ignores your tweet, rants about something else
• One-Word Oracle — single enigmatic words ("no", "wrong", "mid")

THE TRAINING
Over time, you'll notice the pattern: these replies contain no real argumentation, no substance, no signal. They are pure noise. Once you can spot it in simulation, you'll spot it in the wild.

Named after the gom jabbar from Dune — a test of whether you can master your instincts under pressure.

PRIVACY
• No data collection — the only stored data is your handle and preferences
• No network calls — all content generated offline
• No remote code execution — everything runs locally in your browser
• Open source
